Community Guidelines

Welcome to What Next Mentors! Here are the very top-leve guidelines all memebers of our community must follow.
Our entire Code of Conduct can be found here.

Mentees may not ask mentors for financial support.

Mentors provide guidance and advice, but do not provide financial support. Mentees asking mentors for direct financing will have their accounts deactivated immediately.

Mentors may not charge for their services

What Next Mentors is a 100% free service, where volunteer mentors share their experience and their guaidance. Mentors suggesting that Mentees pay for mentoring services on or off teh site will have their accounts deactivated immediately.

Report prohibited behavior

Both mentors and Mentees are invited to report either of the above prohibited behaviors via the REPORT links throughout the site.
